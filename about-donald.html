If you’re my client, I probably know your shoe size and your mom’s middle name. That’s because I believe that details matter. Advice from someone who knows you well will be more likely to work well for you.


For more than a decade, I have dedicated myself to understanding both the behavior of money and the behavior of people relative to money. That understanding coupled with the heart of a teacher are the foundation of my advice.


As a fee-only, fiduciary advisor and planner, I reject the conflicts of interest of selling financial products and focus only on guiding my clients through the maze of managing investment costs and taxes, insuring against risk, establishing a solid estate plan, funding education, and covering retirement income needs.


In finance, there are market events and there are self-inflicted wounds. With my help, you can avoid the costly errors that are common to those who do it themselves or those who hire financial salespeople who aren’t faithful to the client’s interest.


I’m always open to having a conversation. That’s how every good relationship should begin.


I am a follower of Jesus, life-long Floridian, husband, father of two sons and an FSU graduate.
